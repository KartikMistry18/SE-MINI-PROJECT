ALUMNI INTERFACE

Alumni are an institution's most loyal supporters. Alumni are fundraising prospects. Alumni generate invaluable word-of-mouth marketing among their social and professional networks. By engaging alumni, an institution can continue to benefit from their skills and experience.

ALUMNI CORNER

Alumni associations offer networking opportunities, which can connect recent graduates with established professionals and help people pursue career changes. Networking can take many forms. Alumni associations might offer online career fairs or host virtual networking events with tips for job interviews. It's all about name recognition, branding and reputation, aspiration, and goals. Alumni serve as historical markers of success. Alumni can be incredibly helpful to campus recruitment, retention, and career preparation efforts.
